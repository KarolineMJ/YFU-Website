Financing

Haven’t you saved up enough of money? Continue reading!

If you haven’t saved up all what it costs to go on an exchange programme, we have a solution for you. In cooperation with Totalbanken we can offer you a cheap loan for full or partial financing of your exchange stay. The offer applies to all individuals and families where a child is or wishes to become an exchange student with YFU. The loan is granted in accordance with Total Bank's general credit rating.
