Who are we?

Youth for Understanding is a non-profit educational exchange organization with national organizations in more than 50+ countries. We want to explore and gain a deeper understanding of different cultures.

The organization humbly started in the United States in 1951 in an effort to heal the wounds of World War II. From the start, we have been united by the belief that full cultural immersion is an effective means to gain the skills needed to thrive in an increasingly multicultural, competitive global society.

We focus on creating an inspiring network for our exchange students to communicate. We also wish to keep the students involved after their exchange through volunteer work.

Volunteer

In YFU, we can always use and highly value the help of all our motivated and passionate volunteers. Whether it's yoga, IT, human relations or something completely different, the most important thing is your interest and your drive to do something with other YFUs. We want you to be part of our team as well and have a place for you to connect with our diverse service opportunities.

If you enjoy working with people, you could work at an orientation or even interview prospective students and host families. If you like planning and organizing, you could work in an administrative department or help implement policy guidelines. Whatever your interests, we have a place for you to connect.
