Who are we?

Youth for Understanding is a non-profit educational exchange organization with national organizations in more than 50+ countries.

Youth for Understanding humbly started in the United States in 1951 in an effort to heal the wounds of World War II. Since its beginning, we have been united by the belief that full cultural immersion is an effective means to gain the skills needed to thrive in an increasingly multicultural, competitive global society.

We focus on creating a network for our exchange students and to continiue keeping them involved through volunteer work.

Financing

Haven’t you saved up enough of money? Continue reading!

If you haven’t saved up all what it costs to go on an exchange programme, we have a solution for you. In cooperation with Totalbanken we can offer you a cheap loan for full or partial financing of your exchange stay. The offer applies to all individuals and families where a child is or wishes to become an exchange student with YFU. The loan is granted in accordance with Total Bank's general credit rating.
