Travels

Do you want to see the world from a new perspective? Even though you are over 18, you can still experience something as special as is to go for an exchange and live with the host family.

Chile

Country with 4 climate zones and 6435 km long coastline enables you to see everything from penguins to beach and ski resorts. According to OECD, the school system in Chile is one of the best in Latin America.

China

Experience the world’s most populous nation as a scholarship student at a Chinese university. Join the class, learn more about the world and yourself!

USA

Community college in the United States is a year when you choose your subjects and compile your academic benefits while living with a genuine American host family.

Paraguay

Like many other Latin American countries, Paraguay is rich in its amazing culture and nature experiences. As a volunteer at Habitat for Humanity or orphanage Casa Cuna, you will get the opportunity for hands-on experience with another world.

Financing

Haven’t you saved up enough of money? Continue reading!

If you haven’t saved up all what it costs to go on an exchange programme, we have a solution for you. In cooperation with Totalbanken we can offer you a cheap loan for full or partial financing of your exchange stay. The offer applies to all individuals and families where a child is or wishes to become an exchange student with YFU. The loan is granted in accordance with Total Bank's general credit rating.
